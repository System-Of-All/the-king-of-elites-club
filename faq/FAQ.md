# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is NFT minting?
NFT minting is the process of publishing a digital asset on a blockchain as a unique token.

## How do I mint?
Follow the mint instructions once release is announced.

## What file types are supported?
Common NFT formats include PNG, JPG, MP4, and 3D files.

## Are there fees?
Blockchain network fees apply depending on the chain used.

## Can I mint multiple NFTs?
Mint rules will be defined at launch.

## Is copyright transferred?
Ownership of the NFT does not automatically transfer full intellectual property rights unless explicitly stated.
